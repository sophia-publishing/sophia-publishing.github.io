  1. The Archer's Revenge
    Destination Infinity

    The Archer's Revenge is an action-masala novel, giving you the experience of eating a home-cooked meal. As you savour the nostalgia, and chew on the familiar places in and around Chennai, you’ll be met with a few surprises that will make you both laugh and cry. In this debut novel, Destination Infinity inspires each of us that we, too, can write—all it takes is a healthy dose of foolhardiness. Enjoy it with your kids and the kid in you!
